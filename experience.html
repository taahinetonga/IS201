About Me

This is what I do.

I am a college student at Brigham Young University. I grew up playing sports throughout all of my childhood. I started with soccer, then gymnastics, basketball, and sticked to wrestling throughout highschool. While I received a few scholarships to wrestle for college, I dropped them to serve my mission. I returned in 2019, and started my education at BYU in 2020. I played for the BYU Women's team and have since taken a break.

I am number 3 of 10 children. The oldest girl of 4 sisters. I am the first of my family to attend Brigham Young University, Provo. I have a brother who recently graduated from BYU-Hawaii, and another one who is attending BYU-Idaho.
